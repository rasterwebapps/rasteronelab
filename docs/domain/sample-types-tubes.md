# Sample Types & Tubes — RasterOneLab LIS

## Tube Color Coding Guide

| Tube Color | Additive | Sample Type | Primary Use | Min Volume |
|-----------|---------|------------|------------|------------|
| Gold / Red-Grey SST | Gel + Clot activator | Serum | Biochemistry, Serology, Hormones | 3 mL |
| Purple / Lavender EDTA | EDTA K2/K3 | Whole Blood / Plasma | Hematology, HbA1c, Blood group | 2 mL |
| Blue (Light) | Sodium Citrate 3.2% | Plasma | Coagulation (PT, APTT, INR) | 1.8 mL (must be full!) |
| Green | Lithium Heparin | Plasma | Emergency biochemistry (faster than SST) | 2 mL |
| Grey | Sodium Fluoride + EDTA | Whole Blood | Glucose (fluoride inhibits glycolysis) | 2 mL |
| Red (no gel) | None | Serum | Cross-match, special chemistry | 5 mL |
| Dark Blue | EDTA / None | Serum/Blood | Trace metals, toxicology | 5 mL |
| Yellow (SPS) | Sodium Polyanethol Sulfonate | Blood | Blood culture | 10 mL |
| Pink | EDTA K2 | Whole Blood | Blood bank, transfusion | 6 mL |
| White | Gel + K2 EDTA | Plasma | Molecular (PCR, viral load) | 2.5 mL |

## Correct Order of Draw

**IMPORTANT**: Must follow order to prevent cross-contamination of additives.

```
1. Blood Cultures (Yellow/SPS)
2. Coagulation (Blue/Citrate)
3. Serum / SST (Gold/Red)
4. Heparin (Green)
5. EDTA (Purple/Lavender/Pink)
6. Fluoride/Oxalate (Grey)
```

## Sample Stability (Room Temperature vs Refrigerated)

| Sample | Test | RT Stable | 4°C Stable |
|--------|------|-----------|-----------|
| Serum | Glucose | 8 hours | 72 hours |
| Serum | LFT | 24 hours | 7 days |
| Serum | Lipids | 24 hours | 7 days |
| Serum | Thyroid | 8 hours | 5 days |
| EDTA blood | CBC | 24 hours | 48 hours |
| EDTA blood | HbA1c | 7 days | 30 days |
| Citrate plasma | PT/INR | 4 hours | Not recommended |
| Urine | Routine | 2 hours | 24 hours |
| CSF | All | Must be processed immediately | 24 hours (refrigerated) |

## Sample Rejection Criteria

| Condition | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Hemolyzed (red/pink serum) | Reject for K+, LDH, AST; Accept for most others |
| Clotted EDTA tube | Reject ALL — recollect |
| Insufficient volume | Reject — recollect |
| Wrong tube | Reject — recollect |
| Unlabeled tube | Reject — never process |
| Expired tube | Reject — recollect |
| Lipemic (milky serum) | Accept with comment; affects optical tests |
| Icteric (yellow serum) | Accept with comment; affects optical tests |
| > 2 hours old (coagulation) | Reject — recollect |
